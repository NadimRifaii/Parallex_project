Superman

Superman is an alien from the planet Krypton who was sent to Earth as a baby by his parents just before Krypton was destroyed. He was raised by a farm couple in Smallville, Kansas, and learned to use his superpowers for good. As an adult, he moved to the city of Metropolis, where he works as a journalist for the Daily Planet while also protecting the city as Superman.

Super Powers:

  • Superhuman Strength
  • Speed
  • X-ray vision
  • Heat vision

Batman

Batman is the secret identity of Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, who resides in Gotham City. Batman's origin story features him swearing vengeance against criminals after witnessing the murder of his parents Thomas and Martha as a child, a vendetta tempered with the ideal of justice.

Super Powers:

  • Detective Skills
  • Fighting Skills
  • Advanced Technology
